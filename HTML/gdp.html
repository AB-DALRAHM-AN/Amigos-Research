GDP

    GROSS DOMESTIC PRODUCT (GDP):

  • The GDP measures the market value of all final goods and services produced in an economy within one year. This includes all commodities, even if the produced commodities belong to foreign companies, because they were produced within the countrys borders and with its resources.
